Noracle is a tool for question-based dialogue. The Noracle Space starts with a seed question, which is any highly relevant, complex question that has no identifiable answer but which is critical for your community.

Contributors in the Noracle Space “respond” to this seed question with related follow-up questions that can help to unpack or further explore the seed question. Contributors can also respond to any follow-up questions with another question.

Noracle's purpose is to uncover and represent community ignorance: the things we do not yet know and the questions we need to answer. As such, the first rule is: you may only ask questions in this tool. You may not provide any answers or answers formulated as questions (e.g.. "wouldn't it be nice if you did _____?").

If you feel you have an answer, think back to what question might have helped you to arrive at that answer and add that to the Noracle Space.

Noracle is non-hierarchical. In a traditional question-answer paradigm, you have experts and novices and the contributions of each group tend to be asymmetric. In Noracle, novices and experts can contribute side-by-side.
